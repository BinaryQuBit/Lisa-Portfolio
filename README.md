<p>
  The objective of this project was to create a comprehensive and professional portfolio website for a fictional character, Lisa Simpson, using WordPress. The website aims to showcase Lisa Simpson's skills, achievements, and experiences in a visually appealing and user-friendly manner. This project was designed to enhance my understanding of WordPress, including its themes, plugins, and customization options, while also applying best practices in web design and development. The final website serves as a demonstration of my ability to create a functional and aesthetically pleasing online portfolio that effectively communicates the professional persona and capabilities of Lisa Simpson.
</p>

<br>

</div>

<br>

<p>
  Lisa Simpson, a well-known character from the animated television series "The Simpsons," was chosen as the subject of the portfolio to provide a creative and engaging challenge. The aim was to design a site that could serve as a professional online presence for Lisa, highlighting her various talents, accomplishments, and fictional career milestones.
</p>
